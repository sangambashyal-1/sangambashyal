About me

Hey! I'm Sangam Bashyal, a student from Kathmandu, Nepal who's obsessed with building things on the internet. I started with "hello world" and haven't stopped since.

I'm currently studying while simultaneously building projects, reading docs at midnight, and convincing myself that one more feature is totally necessary before shipping.

When the laptop's closed, you'll find me hiking Kathmandu's trails, drinking too much chiya ☕, or falling down interesting Wikipedia rabbit holes.
